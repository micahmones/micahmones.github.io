QuikPix

QuikPix is a Software as a Service product that simplifies the process of mass image conversion and image resizing.

I worked in a team with 3 other individuals (2 designers and 1 other developer) where I worked as a developer, building reusable and functional front-end components for the application.

Built with React.js, HTML/SASS/JavaScript.
